Manual regression checklist (ghost-busters)

Deposit
- Enter hub deposit zone with items; items peel and fly to center; credits apply on last item.
- Start deposit, walk out of zone before finish: partial credit for deposited items, remaining items return to stack (abort).

Hub welcome banner
- First time: enter deposit zone then leave — north welcome banner should hide.
- Optional: never step in deposit — confirm whether persistent banner is acceptable for your design.

Traps
- Slow field: movement slows while inside.
- Damage field: lose stack fraction on first entry per visit; leave and re-enter — damage can fire again.

Upgrade pads (hub)
- After each door unlock (1–4), stand on the matching pad and pay chunks until level advances; labels and occupancy update.

Quest / doors
- Pay doors in order; quest HUD matches spec; blue-gem line when relevant.

Ghost / hunt
- Hunt charge drains while holding; ghosts flee when active; eat and hit behaviors feel correct.
